ABOUT US :

Learn, Invest, Save, Achieve
student Money Mentor is built for students who have goals but no clear way to save for them.
Here, you don't invest to get money back. You invest to buy what you really want later - A new laptop, phone, course or notebook.
Think of it as your digital piggy bank with a goal. You set a target, save money bit by bit, and when you reach it,
You spend it directly on your goal. No returns, No confusion, Just smart saving.
Our mission : Help student build the habits of saving and achieve their first big purchase !

Student Money Mentor helps students develop smart financial habits by managing expenses, saving regularly, tracking goals, and learning financial literacy
